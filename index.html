About

She has plentiful hands-on experience in the design, synthesis, characterization and evaluation of nanomateirals with precisely controlled structures and properties, extensively utilized in heterogenous catalysis and have great potentials as energy-storage battery materials.

Research

My research mainly focus on the design, synthesis and characterization of solid-state catalysts including mono/inter-metallic, precisou/non-precious materials with precisely controlled physicochemical properties, such as different compositions, tunable particle size, various structures (core-shell, homogenous alloys, single atoms), multi-functional sites, etc. The prepared catalysts were evaluated by different catalytic reactions, such as CO combustion, biomass conversion, primary alcohol oxidation, isobutane dehydrogenation, etc. Reaction mechanism, catalytic kinetics, and surface-activity-property function was invstigated.
